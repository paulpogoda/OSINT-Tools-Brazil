# OSINT-Tools-Brazil
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/0/05/Flag_of_Brazil.svg" alt="BRAZIL_FLAG"/>
A list of OSINT resources and tools that may be useful to you when conducting investigations related to Brazil. Most of the resources and tools have versions in Portuguese.

Based on the [OSINT Brazuca Project](https://github.com/osintbrazuca/osint-brazuca). Rearranged and updated for [OSINT For Countries V2.0](https://github.com/paulpogoda/OSINT-for-countries-V2.0). Feel free to join!

## Open Data portals
- [Dados Abertos Gov BR](https://dados.gov.br/home) - Official open data portal from the Brazilian Federal Government
- [Brasil.io](https://brasil.io/datasets/) - Repository of public data made available in accessible format
- [Dados MMA](https://dados.mma.gov.br/) - Environmental data from the Ministry of Environment
- [Dados Ibict](https://dados.ibict.br/dataset) - Open data repository from the Brazilian Institute of Information in Science and Technology
- [Dados BCB](https://dadosabertos.bcb.gov.br/dataset) - Open data portal of the Central Bank of Brazil
- [Dados BNDES](https://dadosabertos.bndes.gov.br/dataset) - National Bank for Economic and Social Development datasets
- [Dados MAPA](https://dados.agricultura.gov.br/dataset) - Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock datasets
- [Dados IBAMA](https://dadosabertos.ibama.gov.br/en/dataset/) - Brazilian Institute of Environment datasets

### Courts and Judicial System
- [Dados Abertos TSE](https://dadosabertos.tse.jus.br/) - Electoral data from Superior Electoral Court
- [DadosJusBr](https://dadosjusbr.org/) - Structured database (2018-2025) of salaries and benefits of over 120 Brazilian Justice system organs
- [JusBrasil](https://www.jusbrasil.com.br/) - Legal proceedings search
- [Escavador](https://www.escavador.com/) - People, institutions and lawsuits search
- [Portal e-SAJ](https://esaj.tjsp.jus.br/esaj/portal.do?servico=190090) - Judicial processes portal (available for multiple states)
- [BNMP - National Database of Arrest Warrants](https://portalbnmp.cnj.jus.br/#/pesquisa-peca) - Centralized system for arrest warrants
- [ABRAJI - Publique-se](https://www.publique-se.org.br/) - Public interest judicial processes involving politicians

### Government and State Resources
- [Portal da Transparência](http://www.portaltransparencia.gov.br/) - Transparency portal for government spending
- [Querido Diário](https://queridodiario.ok.org.br) - Official gazettes from hundreds of Brazilian municipalities
- [State Data Portals](https://dados.ma.gov.br/) - Various states have their own data portals (MA, MG, SC, PE, etc.)

## Legal Entities
- [Receita Federal CNPJ Lookup](http://servicos.receita.fazenda.gov.br/Servicos/cnpjreva/Cnpjreva_Solicitacao.asp) - Official CNPJ (company registration) search
- [BrasilCNPJ](https://brasilcnpj.net/) - CNPJ data search service
- [CNPJ.biz](https://cnpj.biz/) - CNPJ search platform
- [CadastroEmpresa](https://cadastroempresa.com.br/) - Business registry search
- [Casa dos Dados](https://casadosdados.com.br/) - Business information platform
- [CNPJs.rocks](https://cnpjs.rocks/) - CNPJ search tool
- [Rede CNPJ](https://www.redecnpj.com.br/rede/) - Tool for visualizing relationships between companies and partners

### Commercial Registries
- [Junta Comercial/JUCESP](https://www.jucesponline.sp.gov.br/) - São Paulo State Commercial Registry
- [Consulta MEI](http://www22.receita.fazenda.gov.br/inscricaomei/private/pages/certificado_acesso.jsf) - Individual Microentrepreneur registry query
- [Simples Nacional Declaration](http://www8.receita.fazenda.gov.br/SimplesNacional/Aplicacoes/ATSPO/dasnsimei.app/Default.aspx) - Tax declaration for small businesses

### Professional Councils
- [CNA - National Lawyers Registry](https://cna.oab.org.br) - Lawyer registry (OAB)
- [CFM - Federal Medical Council](https://portal.cfm.org.br/busca-medicos/) - Doctor registry
- [CFP - Federal Council of Psychology](https://cadastro.cfp.org.br) - Psychologist registry
- [Confea - Federal Council of Engineering and Agronomy](https://consultaprofissional.confea.org.br) - Engineer registry

## Maps
### Land and Environmental
- [INPE Satellite Images](http://www.dgi.inpe.br/catalogo/) - National Institute for Space Research satellite imagery
- [SICAR-CAR](https://www.car.gov.br/publico/imoveis/index) - National Rural Environmental Registry System
- [IPHAN Heritage Mapping](https://sicg.iphan.gov.br/sicg/pesquisarBem) - National Historical and Artistic Heritage Institute
- [SiBBr - Brazilian Biodiversity Information System](https://collectory.sibbr.gov.br/collectory/datasets) - Biodiversity and ecosystem data
- [Geological Service of Brazil](https://www.sgb.gov.br/) - Geological mapping and resources

### Weather and Climate
- [INPE Fire Monitoring](https://queimadas.dgi.inpe.br/queimadas/portal-static/situacao-atual/) - Real-time fire monitoring
- [REDEMET](https://redemet.decea.gov.br/) - Aeronautics Command Meteorology Network

### Military and Transportation
- [BDGEx](https://bdgex.eb.mil.br/bdgexapp/mobile/) - Army Geographic Database
- [IBGE Cities and States](https://www.ibge.gov.br/cidades-e-estados/) - Statistical data by location

## Vehicles
### Vehicle Registration and Information
- [Placa Lookup Services](https://carfacts.com.br/ConsultaGratis) - License plate lookup
- [FIPE Data](https://veiculos.fipe.org.br) - Vehicle pricing and information database
- [Bicycle Registration (São Paulo)](https://www.ssp.sp.gov.br/consultabicicleta) - Stolen bicycle verification system

### Public Transportation
- [SPTrans Terminals](https://www.sptrans.com.br/terminais) - São Paulo public transport terminals information
- [Ônibus Brasil](https://onibusbrasil.com/placas) - Historical records of buses
- [ANTT Vehicle Search](https://appweb1.antt.gov.br/scff/conPlaca.asp) - National Land Transportation Agency vehicle database
- [Bus Line Search](https://portal.antt.gov.br/linhas-de-onibus) - Intercity bus routes
- [BuscaOnibus](https://www.buscaonibus.com.br/) - Bus schedules and tickets search

### Real-time Tracking
- [Onibus.info](https://onibus.info) - Real-time bus tracking in Joinville
- [SPTrans Olho Vivo](https://olhovivo.sptrans.com.br) - Real-time bus tracking in São Paulo
- [URBS Monitoring](https://www.urbs.curitiba.pr.gov.br/mobile/itibus) - Curitiba public transport tracking
- [Ouro e Prata Tracking](https://rastreamento.viacaoouroeprata.com.br/) - Bus journey tracking
- [Águia Branca Tracking](https://acompanheaviagem.aguiabranca.com.br) - Bus journey tracking

### Aviation and Maritime
- [License Verification ANAC](https://sistemas.anac.gov.br/consultadelicencas/) - Aircraft license verification
- [RAB Consultation](https://sistemas.anac.gov.br/aeronaves/cons_rab.asp) - Brazilian Aeronautical Registry
- [Infraero Flight Consultation](http://voos.infraero.gov.br/voos/index.aspx) - Flight information
- [FAB Authority Flights](https://www.fab.mil.br/voos) - Brazilian authorities flight information
- [ANTAQ Datasets](https://aquarela.antaq.gov.br/) - National Agency for Waterway Transportation data

## People
### Personal Data and Background
- [CPF/CNPJ Name Lookup](https://www.situacao-cadastral.com/) - CPF/CNPJ registration status
- [Criminal Background Check](https://antecedentes.policiacivil.pa.gov.br/consulta) - Criminal records search
- [Income Tax Refund](http://solucoes.receita.fazenda.gov.br/Servicos/ConsRest/Atual.app/paginas/mobile/restituicaoMobi.asp) - Tax refund information
- [Benefit Decision Communication](https://www2.dataprev.gov.br/sabiweb/relatorio/imprimirCRER.view?acao=imprimir_CRER) - Retirement request status (INSS)

### Missing Persons and Civil Identification
- [Missing Persons Databases](https://desaparecidos.pcivil.rj.gov.br/pesquisar) - Multiple state databases for missing persons
- [São Paulo Missing Persons](https://www.ssp.sp.gov.br/servicos/desaparecidos) - São Paulo state database
- [Death Records](https://www.falecidosnobrasil.org.br/) - Brazilian death records
- [Birth, Death and Marriage Statistics](https://transparencia.registrocivil.org.br/registros) - Civil registry transparency portal

### Academics and Research
- [Lattes CV Database](https://buscatextual.cnpq.br/buscatextual/busca.do?metodo=apresentar) - Academic curriculum database
- [Higher Education Institutions](https://emec.mec.gov.br/emec/nova#avancada) - Ministry of Education database of institutions
- [CNES - Health Facilities Registry](https://cnes.datasus.gov.br/) - Information about health establishments

### Telecom and Device Information
- [Telephone Operator Lookup](https://consultanumero.info/) - Mobile carrier identification
- [Number Portability](https://consultanumero.abrtelecom.com.br/consultanumero/consulta/consultaSituacaoAtualCtg) - Phone number portability check
- [IMEI Check](https://www.consultaaparelhoimpedido.com.br/public-web/welcome) - Mobile device verification
- [Pre-paid Lines by CPF](https://cadastropre.com.br/#/consulta) - Check active pre-paid lines

### Social Media and Messaging
- [WhatsApp Groups Index](https://gruposwhats.app/) - WhatsApp group directories
- [Telegram Groups Search (Brazil focus)](https://tgstat.com/search) - Telegram channel and group search with Brazil focus

### Social Benefits
- [Social Benefits Consultation](https://www.beneficiossociais.caixa.gov.br/consulta/beneficio/04.01.00-00_00.asp) - Bolsa Família program query
- [Emergency Aid Consultation](https://consultaauxilio.cidadania.gov.br/consulta/#/) - COVID-19 emergency aid verification

## Public procurement
- [Alerta Licitação](https://alertalicitacao.com.br/) - Bidding alert service
- [Portal de Compras Públicas](https://www.portaldecompraspublicas.com.br/18/Processos/) - Public procurement portal
- [COVID-19 Spending Monitor](https://www.tesourotransparente.gov.br/visualizacao/painel-de-monitoramentos-dos-gastos-com-covid-19) - Monitoring of pandemic-related expenditures
- [SPTrans Bidding Processes](https://sptrans.com.br/licitacoes/) - São Paulo transport company procurement
- [Government Procurement Portal](https://www.gov.br/compras/pt-br/painel-covid) - Federal government procurement platform
- [Transparency Portal COVID-19](https://portaldatransparencia.gov.br/coronavirus) - COVID-19 related expenditures

## WHOIS
- [Registro.br](https://registro.br/tecnologia/ferramentas/whois/) - Brazilian domain registry WHOIS tool
- [RDAP Queries](https://rdap.registro.br/domain/) - Registration Data Access Protocol for .br domains
- [Domain Process List](https://registro.br/dominio/lista-processo-liberacao.txt) - List of domain release processes
- [User Search](https://registro.br/tecnologia/ferramentas/pesquisa-de-usuario/) - Domain user search tool
- [BR Domain Categories](https://registro.br/dominio/categorias/) - Categories of .br domains
- [ASN Information](https://ftp.registro.br/pub/numeracao/origin/nicbr-asn-blk-latest.txt) - ASN information from Registro.br
